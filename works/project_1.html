Sword of Destiny

A short animation project with the only prompt being the titles name: "Sword of Destiny".




Prompt
Create a short animation using the assets provided or make your own (I made my own). The title has to be "Sword of Destiny"

Idea
Inspired by a sword in a game I was playing at the time. I wanted to make a story of blood from a unknown source (perhaps a war that created the pools of blood?) to awaken the sword from its slumber.

Tools used
Adobe Illustrate and After Affects

Reflection
I would want to put more details and make the animation smoother with what I learned while creating this project
